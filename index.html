A little about me

Hello everyone, my name is Ron Shmuel Cohen and I am 26 years old, I was born and I live in Israel. Working in the field of quality assurance, I'm highly motivation for learning and development, solving problems while paying attention to the smallest details. Before I moved to the Quality Assurance, I worked in detailing for luxury cars and also was worked in finishing work in private homes. In addition, on this page I will show you my sporting hobby - snowboarding.
As a person with medical issue like asthma I found this sport to be perfect for me as no cardiopulmonary endurance is required. You are welcome to take a look and see the sites I visited.

Austria - Mayrhofen 19'-20'

Mayrhofen stands in a valley in the Tyrol region at the height of the Austrian Alps. Ancient buildings that wrap an 800-year-old tradition, a friendly pedestrian street for shopping enthusiasts, traditional food and more. Above the town rise ridges at heights between 2,000 meters and 3,500 meters that are divided into 5 different skiing areas where everyone can find the style that suits them.
I have vacationed year after year in the exact same destination, for me it is the ultimate destination for all levels of Snowborading, and not just for skiing, it is a destination in general suitable for tourism and nightlife, I have no doubt that I will return again!


Bulgaria - Bansko 23'

As of 2023, Bansko is the last ski resort I visited. From a personal opinion, I must say that this site is suitable for people with no skiing/snowboarding experience or people who are on their first vacation because this site does not offer many long skiing routes. So why did I decide to go anyway? I managed to convince few friends to come and try snowboarding for the first time - which turned out to be the perfect destination for them! But on the other hand, the ski town of Bansko is very suitable for the winter season, endless restaurants and nightlife, everything is close to each other and there are definitely more things to do besides skiing.


Israel - Mt. Hermon

The Mount Hermon site is a tourist, vacation and recreation site that is active all year round. The mountain covers an area of about 2,434 dunams, at an altitude of 1,600 to 2,040 meters above sea level. This is the ski resort where i tried snowboarding for the first time, I must say it was not easy! A lot of painful falls and difficulty in absorbing the idea of snowboarding. Although compared to other mountains, Mount Hermon is very small in terms of area and the length of the routes. But when we travel for a whole day of snowboarding, we know in advance that we will have a lot of fun!
